陈科海 (Chen, Kehai)

I have been a professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, China, since October 2023. My research interests focus on Natural Language Processing, AI Agents, and Multi-modal generation. I have published more than 90 papers in top-tier NLP/ML/AI conferences and journals, such as ACL, NeurIPS, ICLR, AAAI, IJCAI, TPAMI, TASLP, etc.
